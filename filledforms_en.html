About the Questionnaire

The morphosyntactic questionnaire was developed by project participants for systematic description of control and raising constructions in the languages of Eurasia. The questionnaire includes 12 main sections:

  1. Clause boundaries and multiplicity of description
  2. Word order, clause structure and clitics
  3. Control and raising constructions
  4. Syntactic domain of raising
  5. Biclausal structures and restructuring
  6. Morphosyntax and argument structure
  7. Morphosyntactic type of dependent predication
  8. Transparent agreement
  9. Indexical shift
  10. Actant derivation
  11. Syntactic constructions of different verbal classes
  12. External possessor
